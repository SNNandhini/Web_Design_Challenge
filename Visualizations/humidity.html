Humidity

The humidity% seem to be high at the equator (latitude of 0). It also seem to be high at the extreme north or south (poles). However, there is no particular trend with the increase or decrease of the latitude values. So there may or may not be any relationship between humidity and latitude. Further tests like correlation coefficient is required to confirm this.
